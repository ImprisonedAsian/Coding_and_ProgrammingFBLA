How to Use the Finance Tracker

1. Adding a Transaction:
- Enter a description, amount, category, and date.
- Click "Add Transaction" to save it.

2. Editing a Transaction:
- Click the "Edit" button next to a transaction.
- Modify the details in the form and click "Save Changes".

3. Deleting a Transaction:
- Click the "X" button next to a transaction to delete it.

4. Searching Transactions:
- Use the search bar to filter transactions by description.

5. Generating Reports:
- Click "Show Summary" to see a pie chart of expenses by category.
- Click "Weekly Summary" or "Monthly Summary" to see a line graph of your balance over time.

6. FAQ:
- Click the "FAQ" button to see this guide anytime!
